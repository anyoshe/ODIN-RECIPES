DEB'S PASSOVER BROWNIES

Description

Very chocolaty and tasty brownies for Passover- they do not taste like they should be kosher for the holiday, but they are!!!

Per Serving: 173 calories; protein 2g; carbohydrates 22g; fat 9.9g; cholesterol 38.5mg; sodium 91.4mg. Full Nutrition

  • 4 (1 ounce) squares unsweetened baking chocolate
  • 1 cup butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 egg whites
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • ¼ cup strong brewed coffee
  • 2 teaspoons kosher for Passover vanilla flavoring
  • 1 cup potato starch
  • 2 tablespoons potato starch
  • ¼ cup matzo cake meal
  • 2 tablespoons matzo cake meal
  • ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 6 ounces semisweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Step 1
    2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease 2 8x8-inch baking pans.
  2. Step 2
    3. Melt unsweetened chocolate and butter in the top of a double boiler over simmering water; stir to mix well. Beat the eggs and egg whites together in a mixing bowl with an electric mixer on high speed until very fluffy, about 5 minutes. Gently stir the butter and chocolate mixture, sugar, coffee, and vanilla flavoring into the egg mixture, and beat well to combine.
  3. Step 3
    4. Mix together all of the potato starch, all of the matzo cake meal, cocoa powder, and salt in a bowl. Beat the dry ingredients into the chocolate mixture with electric mixer until fluffy and thoroughly combined. Gently fold the chocolate chips into the batter, and divide between the 2 prepared baking dishes.
  4. Step 4
    5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of a pan shows moist crumbs, 25 to 30 minutes.

Enjoy your meal
